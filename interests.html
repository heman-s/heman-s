Interests

Sports

At a young age, I would participate in sports such as football, basketball and swimming in a casual setting. On joining secondary school, I continued playing basketball through training sessions during lunch times and on occasion represented my school through games against other schools. In my later years of secondary school, I stopped basketball to try and discover a new sport and so I represented a local club to play rugby for around 2 years. On joining university, I returned to playing basketball as well as working out in the gym. Again, I wanted to try something new, so whilst participating in these activities, I also started dancing sessions with my university.

Music

During secondary school, I started learning to play the piano at school during lessons and would also practice at home. Currently, I am teaching myself to play the guitar through the various chords and through different songs. I would like to keep a consistent routine to consolidate my musical ability in guitar and piano in my own time.

I also like to sing, having previously performed for my secondary school Christmas choir.

Art

Since young, I have always liked to draw. I pursued Art for GCSE and created various pieces of work.

One of my main themes during my GCSE was the idea of augmented humans and how technology can be integrated into us.
